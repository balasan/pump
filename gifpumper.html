Gifpumper is a communal image collage. All changes are seen by everyone
instantaneously, enabling realtime collaboration between many users.

Instructions:

Move image: click and drag

Resize: Shift-click and drag

Move forward/back: Alt-click and drag up/down

Rotate around the Z axes: hold Z key & click and drag left/right

Rotate around the X/Y axes: hold X key & click and drag

Delete image: first click on image (drag it to make sure) then Cntrl X

Page Management

Register: click Login and enter a unique username and password and hit Register

Create a New Page: go to More Pages enter new page name and hit New Page

Save Current Page: go to to More Pages, enter a new name and hit Save As

set Privacy levels of a page you created in Page Settings/br>
Tips


most menus can be accessed by hitting Shift and the first letter of the menu

replace and delete will effect the last image that was clicked/dragged
